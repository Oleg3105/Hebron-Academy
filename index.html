Making Goodness Together

Social Project for Orphans and Unprivileged Children

A huge question arises once orphans in Ukraine turn 18, graduate from school and approach their adulthood.

These children usually turn out to be not adapted to independent life. After entering low-level colleges, they are not motivated to pay proper attention to education. Orphaned, abandoned, and underprivileged, they come across various difficulties and are inclined to waste their lives.

Hebron desires to provide children in need with life-changing opportunities so that they would become independent and contributing members of the community.

Our Process

How Academy Works

Pre-Selection

We admit boarding school graduates, orphans and impoverished children who want to become IT specialists and are ready to change their lives. Admission is available over the entire year.

Studying

The student follows a comprehensive twenty four - month program targeting at spiritual, intellectual, and physical development.

Employment

The final goal of this social project is to provide a student with a Hebron graduation certificate that verifies the knowledge of the junior specialist in the chosen IT field. We help students to get employed in their Technology field.
