About Us

After returning from San Francisco in 2015, the young couple of Ihor Bula and Liliya Valihun decided to open an IT School for orphans. The establishment of Hebron is the result of the long-term commitment to help orphans integrate into society through intellectual growth and spiritual consolidation.

Because of some difficulties, connected with the work with the youth of social category, the decision was made to reorient this project towards Christianity. We do social rehabilitation and integration into society on the basis of biblical values.

This promotes not only the formation of the high-quality educational environment but also turns Hebron IT Academy into the spiritual family.
