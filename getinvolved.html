How to Help Us

We currently exist solely on the support of financial donations.

We require monthly sponsors for each student, and also we need general donations to fill shortfalls when sponsors are not found, as well as when building maintenance and additional expenses are required.

No donation is too small and every contribution matters! In addition to financial support, we need volunteer support for teaching, mentoring, and being a friends to our students. Above all, we need your prayers!

100 sponsors from Ukraine, USA, Canada, and other nations around the globe join together, making monthly donations of $50 each. These regular donations will help provide dependable regular funding to develop and support Hebron Education Charity Organization based in Ukraine.

Mission

Our goal is to provide free education, mentorship, personal development, life skills, internship in IT and employment for orphans and children from low-income families. The primary goal is to help them find their place in society and to know God.

Vision

To be the center of innovative education among orphans and underprivileged youth. It will be combined with Biblical upbringing, online education and new resources
